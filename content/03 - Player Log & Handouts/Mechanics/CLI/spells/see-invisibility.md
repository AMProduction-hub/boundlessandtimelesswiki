# See Invisibility
*2nd-level, Divination*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, you see [invisible](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.
