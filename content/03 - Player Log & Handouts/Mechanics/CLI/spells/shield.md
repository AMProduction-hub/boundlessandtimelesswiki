# Shield
*1st-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from [magic missile](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/magic-missile.md).
