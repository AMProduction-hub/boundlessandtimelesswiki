# Lesser Restoration
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be [blinded](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [deafened](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [paralyzed](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [poisoned](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned).
