# Charm of Darkvision

This charm allows you to cast the [darkvision](03%20-%20Player%20Log%20&%20Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/darkvision.md) spell as an action, no components required. Once used three times, the charm goes away.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228*
