## Campaign Overview

A short-form campaign designed to explore the **Daggerheart** system by Darrington Press. Set in a mysterious jungle connected to Jyggalag, players experienced a different kind of TTRPG system while still maintaining narrative depth.

This campaign served as both an adventure and a learning experience for trying out new mechanics:
- Card-based resolution system
- Different approach to character creation
- Unique stress and hope mechanics
- Collaborative storytelling elements

---

## What Made It Special

**System Exploration**: Testing Daggerheart's unique mechanics in practice.

**Short & Focused**: A complete story arc in limited sessions.

**Jungle Mystery**: Atmospheric setting with secrets to uncover.

**Learning Together**: GM and players discovering the system together.

## Daggerheart Highlights

**Hope & Fear**: The dual-currency system created interesting decision points.

**Collaborative Success**: The system encouraged more player-GM collaboration.

**Different Pace**: Combat and challenges felt distinct from D&D 5e.

**Character Depth**: Backstory integration worked smoothly with mechanics.
