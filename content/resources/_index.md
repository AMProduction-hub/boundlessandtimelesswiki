## Quick Rules Reference

### Common Actions in Combat
- **Attack**: Roll d20 + Attack Modifier vs. AC
- **Cast a Spell**: Check spell components, range, and casting time
- **Dodge**: Attackers have disadvantage, you have advantage on DEX saves
- **Help**: Give an ally advantage on their next ability check or attack
- **Hide**: Make a Dexterity (Stealth) check
- **Ready**: Prepare an action for a specific trigger

### Common Conditions
- **Prone**: Disadvantage on attacks, attacks against you have advantage
- **Grappled**: Speed becomes 0
- **Restrained**: Disadvantage on attacks and DEX saves, attacks against you have advantage
- **Stunned**: Can't move or take actions, attacks against you have advantage
- **Paralyzed**: Auto-fail STR and DEX saves, attacks against you have advantage and crit within 5 feet

## Session Zero Materials

For new campaigns or new players:

### Session Zero Checklist
- [ ] Character creation guidelines
- [ ] Safety tools discussion (Lines & Veils)
- [ ] House rules overview
- [ ] Campaign tone and expectations
- [ ] Party dynamics and roles
- [ ] Scheduling and attendance

### Character Creation Checklist
- [ ] Choose race and class
- [ ] Roll or point-buy ability scores
- [ ] Select background and starting equipment
- [ ] Write backstory and heroic feat
- [ ] Choose personality traits, ideals, bonds, flaws
- [ ] Review house rules for any modifications

| Waterdeep | Baldur's Gate | ~750 miles | 30-37 days on foot |

*Travel times assume normal pace (24 miles/day on foot, 40 miles/day mounted)*

### Resting Rules
- **Short Rest**: 1 hour, spend Hit Dice to regain HP, regain some class features
- **Long Rest**: 8 hours, regain all HP and Hit Dice (up to half your total), regain spell slots
