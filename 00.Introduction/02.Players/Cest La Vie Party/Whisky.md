# Whisky
## Overview
### Basic Background
- **Who are you?** (Name, race, class, and a defining trait)
>Old Name : Sinyo, New(isekai'd) : Meows Whiskerpop (Whisky), race tabaxi, trait: curious, naive
- **Where are you from?** (Hometown, culture, or region)
    From earth, isekai'd to baldurs gate
- **What do you look like?** (Distinctive features, clothing, or demeanor)
    A Grey colored cat with white socks motive

**Personal History (Shaping Events)**

- **What was your life before adventuring?** (Occupation, family, or past experiences)
    She was a house cat from modern earth with an owner named Rhea that are a fangirl of a kpop grups. Motivating her to be an idol but unable to do so because she cant dances/moves or sings like a human. She died after protecting her house from a burglar. She then got offered by a god (she so she thought (ill leave it to dm hehehehe)) to live in another world and fulfill her dream as an idol with a more proper body (like tabaxi) in exchange that she do an errand(or two) of the supposed god. As a naive cat, she agreed and then get transported into baldurs gate. (She also got her new name from her supposed god)
- **What is your biggest regret or failure?** (A past mistake or loss)
    not taking down the burglar (killing him), because she now doesnt know the condition of her owner, Rhea.
- **What is your greatest accomplishment?** (A proud moment or success)
    Making her owner laughing with her supposed dances(moves) and sings(meows)

**Character Motivation (Why You Adventure)**

- **What do you seek?** (Glory, redemption, power, knowledge, etc.)
    The same smile in her future fans that Rhea shows when she watch her kpop groups perform
- **What are you running from?** (A past enemy, mistake, or burden)
    Cat hater, cat abuser
- **Who or what do you hold dear?** (Family, friends, a mentor, or an ideal)
	Rhea
