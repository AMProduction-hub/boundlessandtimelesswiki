# Father Malen
## Overview
Father Malen is the current head of House of Silence in Shattar-Kai a temple dedicated to [[faerunian-kelemvor-scag|The Lord of the Dead - Kelemvor]]. Before he became head of House of Silence Father Malen once was a famous tailor in this lands and had [[Nikael of Fire Banner]] as his apprentice. His view of the world quite idealistic he want every people to have peace and be together. Now his works is overflow by sending men and women to their grave caused the wars that happen caused of [[Fremen]] & [[Stalwart]].
