# Grimlock
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 282*  


- **Ability Scores**: Strength +2; Charisma -2
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft.

## Traits

### Blindsight

You have no eyes, and cannot be [blinded](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded). You can perceive your surroundings within 30 ft., or 10 ft. while [deafened](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Deafened). You are considered blind beyond this radius.

### Keen Hearing and Smell

You have advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

### Stone Camouflage

You have advantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write Undercommon.
