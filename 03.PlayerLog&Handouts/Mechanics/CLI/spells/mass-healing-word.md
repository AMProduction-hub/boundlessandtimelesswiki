# Mass Healing Word
*3rd-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

As you call out words of restoration, up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain hit points equal to `d4` + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by `d4` for each slot level above 3rd.
