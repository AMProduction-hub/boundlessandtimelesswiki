# Toll the Dead
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You point at one creature you can see within range, and the sound of a dolorous bell fills the air around it for a moment. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take `d8` necrotic damage. If the target is missing any of its hit points, it instead takes `d12` necrotic damage.

The spell's damage increases by one die when you reach 5th level (`2d8` or `2d12`), 11th level (`3d8` or `3d12`), and 17th level (`4d8` or `4d12`).
