# Gift of Alacrity
*1st-level, Divination*  


- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add `d8` to its initiative rolls.

**Classes**: [Bard](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md)

*Source: Explorer's Guide to Wildemount p. 186*
