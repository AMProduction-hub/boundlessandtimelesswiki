# Sacred Flame
*cantrip, Evocation*  
![](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/img/sacred-flame.webp#right)

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Flame-like radiance descends on a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take `d8` radiant damage. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw.

The spell's damage increases by `d8` when you reach 5th level (`2d8`), 11th level (`3d8`), and 17th level (`4d8`).
