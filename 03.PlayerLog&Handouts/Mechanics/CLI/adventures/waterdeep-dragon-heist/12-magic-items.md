# Magic Items
*Source: Waterdeep: Dragon Heist, p. 189* 

This appendix describes new magic items that appear in the adventure, and which are presented here in alphabetical order.

Several of these items are found in the possession of specific NPCs:

- [Aurinax](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/aurinax-wdh.md) has the [dragonstaff of Ahghairon](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/dragonstaff-of-ahghairon-wdh.md).  
- [Jalester Silvermane](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/jalester-silvermane-wdh.md) has a [badge of the Watch](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/badge-of-the-watch-wdh.md).  
- [Jarlaxle Baenre](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/jarlaxle-baenre-wdh.md) has a [bracer of flying daggers](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/bracer-of-flying-daggers-wdh.md), a [feather of diatryma summoning](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/feather-of-diatryma-summoning-wdh.md), a [knave's eye patch](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/knaves-eye-patch-wdh.md), and a [ring of truth telling](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/ring-of-truth-telling-wdh.md).  
- [Meloon Wardragon](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/meloon-wardragon-wdh.md) has [Azuredge](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/azuredge-wdh.md).  
- [Vajra Safahr](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/vajra-safahr-wdh.md) has the [Blackstaff](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/blackstaff-wdh.md).
