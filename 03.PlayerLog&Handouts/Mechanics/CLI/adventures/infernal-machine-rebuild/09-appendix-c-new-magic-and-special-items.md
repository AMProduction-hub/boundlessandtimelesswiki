# Appendix C: New Magic and Special Items
*Source: Infernal Machine Rebuild, p. 93* 

This chapter details new poisons, spells, and magic items that appear in the adventure—including the legendary artifact *The Infernal Machine of Lum the Mad*.

## Poisons

The artificer [Thessalar](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/thessalar-imr.md) is known for his experiments with unique toxins, three of which can be found in this adventure.

- [Blood of the Lycanthrope](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/blood-of-the-lycanthrope-imr.md)  
- [Dust of the Mummy](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/dust-of-the-mummy-imr.md)  
- [Thessaltoxin](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/items/thessaltoxin-imr.md)
