# Charm of Vicious Mockery

This charm allows you to cast the [vicious mockery](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/vicious-mockery.md) cantrip (5th-level version; save DC 15) as an action. Once used three times, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
