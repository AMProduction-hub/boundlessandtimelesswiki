# Charm of Lightning Bolt

This charm allows you to cast the [lightning bolt](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/lightning-bolt.md) spell (save DC 15) as an action, no components required. Once used, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
