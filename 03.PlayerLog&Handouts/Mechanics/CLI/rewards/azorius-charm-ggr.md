# Azorius Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [hold person](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/hold-person.md) (3rd-level version), [command](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/command.md), or [counterspell](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/counterspell.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 173*
