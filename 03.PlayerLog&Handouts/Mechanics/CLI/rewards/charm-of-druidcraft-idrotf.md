# Charm of Druidcraft

This charm allows you to cast the [druidcraft](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/druidcraft.md) cantrip as an action, no components required. Once used three times, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
