# Charm of Sending

This charm allows you to cast the [sending](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/sending.md) spell as an action, no components required. Once used, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
