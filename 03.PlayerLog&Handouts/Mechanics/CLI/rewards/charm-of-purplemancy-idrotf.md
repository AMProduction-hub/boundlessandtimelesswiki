# Charm of Purplemancy

This charm allows you to cast the [faerie fire](03.PlayerLog&Handouts/Mechanics/CLI/spells/faerie-fire.md) spell (violet light only; save DC 15) as a bonus action instead of an action, no components required. Once used three times, this charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden p. 228*
