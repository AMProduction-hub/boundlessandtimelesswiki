    ðŸŽ¯ My GMing Philosophy#

    Player Agency First: Your choices matter and shape the world.

    Rule of Cool: If it’s awesome and makes sense narratively, we’ll make it work.

    Collaborative Storytelling: We build the story together, not just me telling it to you.

    Meaningful Consequences: Actions have weight, both good and bad.

    Fun Over Rules: The rules serve the story, not the other way around.
