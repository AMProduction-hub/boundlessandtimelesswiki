    Events That Shaped the World#

    These major events have shaken the foundation of Toril and beyond:

    The Blood War#

    “An eternal battle rages in the Lower Planes, but for the first time in recorded history, forces that should be sworn enemies now fight side by side. Why?”

    Devils and demons, locked in eternal conflict, have begun to cooperate. This unprecedented alliance signals something far more terrifying than their eternal war.

    The Time of Troubles#

    “The gods walked Toril as mortals, reshaping faith and power forever. But did Ao truly restore the balance, or did he prepare something even grander?”

    “A city once bathed in holy light fell into the depths of Hell. Many were saved, but the scars left behind tell of a bargain that could repeat itself.”
