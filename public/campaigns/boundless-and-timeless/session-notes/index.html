    How to Use Session Notes#

    Each session note contains:

    • Characters Present: Who played that session
    • Session Overview: What happened (spoiler-free summary at top)
    • Key Learnings: Important information discovered
    • NPCs Met: New characters encountered
    • Detailed Recap: Full session breakdown
