    Campaign Philosophy#

    Each campaign I run has these core principles:

    Player-Driven Stories: Your choices drive the narrative forward.

    Living Worlds: The world continues whether you’re there or not. NPCs have their own goals.

    Consequences Matter: Actions have ripples. Some immediate, some that surface sessions later.

    Multiple Solutions: There’s rarely one “correct” way to solve a problem.

    Tone Consistency: Each campaign has its own tone, from serious to silly.

    **Looking to Join?** If you're interested in joining a future campaign, reach out! I occasionally start new campaigns or have openings in existing ones.
