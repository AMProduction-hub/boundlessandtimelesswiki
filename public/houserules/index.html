    Skills#

    Untuk Skills, player mendapatkan tambahan skills pada level 3 kemudian setiap 2 level sampai batas level. Berikut adalah penjelasannya:

    Level Skill Increases Effect
    3 +1 Skill Gain proficiency in a new skill OR improve an existing skill.
    5 +1 Skill Improve a skill to Expertise Level 1 (double proficiency bonus).
    7 +1 Skill Gain proficiency in a new skill OR improve an existing skill.
    9 +1 Skill Improve a skill to Expertise Level 2 (special effects unlocked).
    11 +1 Skill Gain proficiency in a new skill OR improve an existing skill.
    13 +1 Skill Improve a skill to Expertise Level 3 (powerful effects unlocked).
    15 +1 Skill Gain proficiency in a new skill OR improve an existing skill.
    17 +1 Skill Improve a skill to Expertise Level 4 (legendary effect).
    19 +1 Skill Gain proficiency in a new skill OR improve an existing skill.

    Skill Improvement Explanation#

    Each skill can be improved multiple times to unlock new effects. You can choose to improve a skill instead of gaining a new one at certain levels.

    Skill Progression Tiers:#

    • Expertise Level 1: Double proficiency bonus.
    • Expertise Level 2: Unlocks a minor special effect.
    • Expertise Level 3: Unlocks a major combat or utility effect.
    • Expertise Level 4: Unlocks a legendary effect.

    How This Works in Play#

    • Each level, you choose to either:
      • Gain proficiency in a new skill (if you arenâ€™t proficient already).
      • Improve an existing skill, unlocking new abilities at levels 5, 9, 13, and 17.
    • At level 3 and every two levels after, you get one skill increase.
    • Rogues gain a skill increase every level, making them the undisputed skill masters.

    Armor Class#

    Untuk armor class akan berfungsi sepertinya pada core rules dari DnD 5e. Berikut adalah tambahan tipe Armor Class:

    Touch Armor Class (AC)#

    Touch Armor Class (AC) adalah Armor Class tanpa menambahkan modifier dari armor yang digunakan oleh character tetapi menambahkan modifier dari Dexterity ataupun jika menggunakan Unarmored Defense dari monk maka akan menambahkan modifier dari Wisdom.

    Cara menghitung Touch AC#

    Touch AC = 10 + Dexterity Modifier + Any Dodge/Deflection Bonuses + Size Modifier

    ðŸ”¹ Example Calculations

    Character Normal AC Touch AC Notes
    Fighter in Full Plate (AC 18, Dex +0) 18 10 Heavy armor offers no protection.
    Dexterous Rogue (AC 15, Dex +4, Unarmored) 15 14 Still agile, but bullets are dangerous.
    Monk with Unarmored Defense (AC 17, Dex +3, Wis +2) 17 15 Monk still benefits from speed.
    Tiny Creature (Fairy, Dex +3, Size +2) 16 15 Small size makes them harder to hit.

    Cover Mechanic#

    Player dapat menggunakan cover yang tersedia pada map (battle) yang akan menambahkan Touch AC character yang dapat dilihat dari: $Cover Size$ vs $Character Size$. Berikut adalah attributenya:

    Cover Size Touch AC Bonus
    Half-Cover (Setengah Badan) +2 Touch AC
    3/4 Cover +5 Touch AC
    Total Cover Tidak bisa di-target

    Bonus Action#

    Pada saat combat player dapat melakukan gerakan berikut dengan memakan bonus action mereka. Berikut adalah list dari gerakannya:

    Intimidate#

    Ketika combat player yang jaraknya tidak lebih dari 5ft dari target dapat melakukan Intimidate kepada musuh jika:

    Roll + Intimidate Skills >= 10 + Wisdom Modifier

    maka target akan mendapatkan $-1$ Penalty pada ACRoll target per 1 round.

    Feint#

    Ketika combat player yang jaraknya tidak lebih dari 5ft dari target dapat melakukan Feint untuk mendapatkan advantage jika:

    Roll + Deception >= 10 + Wisdom Modifier

    maka player akan mendapatkan advantage pada target tersebut.

    Consume Potion & Goodberry#

    Saat combat player dapat menggunakan potion dengan memakan bonus action character mereka.

    Spells & Levels Tiers#

    Levels Tiers#

    Di dalam campaign ini akan ditunjukkan tier level yang memungkinkan membantu untuk saat saving throws, berikut adalah list nya :

    Tiers Levels Monster CR Description
    Tier 1 1 - 4 CR 1/8 - CR 4 Local Heroes
    Tier 2 5 - 10 CR 5 - CR 10 Heroes of the Realms
    Tier 3 11 - 16 CR 11 - CR 16 Masters of the Realms
    Tier 4 17 - 20 CR 17+ Masters of the World

    Apa yang mempengaruhi level tiers ini ? Pengaruhnya adalah kepada saving throws kepada karakter, terutama boss jika memiliki tier diatas player. Berikut adalah deskripsi-nya:

    • Powerful enemies (higher tier than the caster) resist full-effect CC spells.

      • Example: A Tier 3 boss vs. a Tier 2 caster â†’ Hold Person wonâ€™t fully work unless the boss fails by 5+.
      • Weaker enemies still get affected normally.

    • Partial effects replace Legendary Resistances:

      • Example: If a boss barely fails a saving throw against Polymorph, it might lose its turn instead of transforming.
      • If they fail by 10+, the full effect applies.

    Berikut adalah spell scaling berdasarkan weakened atau enhanced effects:

    Tier-Based Spell Scaling (Weakened Effects)#

    If the target is a higher tier than the caster, the spell effect is reduced based on its type:

    Spell Type Weakened Effect Against Higher-Tier Creatures
    Save-or-Suck (Paralysis, Polymorph, Banishment, etc.) Partial effect instead of full (Hold Monster only stuns, Polymorph reduces speed & AC instead of transforming, etc.).
    Charm & Mind Control (Dominate, Suggestion, Fear, etc.) The target gets an extra save every round. If they fail by 10 or more, they get the normal effect.
    Damage Spells (Fireball, Disintegrate, etc.) The target resists half the damage if it saves by 5 or more.
    Debuffs (Bane, Slow, Bestow Curse, etc.) Effects are halved (e.g., Slow only reduces movement, Bestow Curse lasts fewer rounds).
    Summoning Spells (Conjure X, Planar Binding, etc.) Summoned creatures have fewer hit points (or weaker effects).
    Control Spells (Wall of Force, Telekinesis, etc.) The target can interact with or resist the spell more easily (e.g., a Tier 4 creature can break Wall of Force with an action).
    Healing & Buffs (Bless, Shield, etc.) No change unless a higher-tier creature is negating them (e.g., an anti-magic field).

    Tier-Based Spell Scaling (Enhanced Effects)#

    If the caster’s tier is higher than the target’s tier, the spell is empowered:

    Spell Type Enhanced Effect Against Lower-Tier Creatures
    Save-or-Suck Spells If the target fails by 10 or more, they suffer double duration or lose their next action.
    Charm & Mind Control No extra saves.
    Damage Spells On a failed save by 10 or more, damage doubles (like a critical hit).
    Debuffs No immunity (e.g., normally Bestow Curse might not affect legendary creatures, but it now does).
    Summoning Spells Summoned creatures have bonus HP and attack rolls.
    Control Spells The spell’s range, duration, or area increases.
    Healing & Buffs Targets gain extra temp HP or resistances.

    Example Applications#

    • A Level 8 Sorcerer (Tier 2) casting Hold Person on a CR 15 Lich (Tier 3) â†’ Instead of full paralysis, the Lich loses its next bonus action on a failed save.

    • A Level 18 Cleric (Tier 4) casting Banishment on a CR 9 Giant (Tier 2) â†’ If the Giant fails by 10 or more, it is banished permanently instead of just for 1 minute.

    • A Level 6 Bard (Tier 2) casting Fear on a CR 20 Balor (Tier 4) â†’ The Balor gets advantage on its save, and on failure, it only loses its next reaction instead of running away.

    • A Level 16 Wizard (Tier 3) casting Fireball on a CR 6 Troll (Tier 2) â†’ If the Troll fails by 10 or more, it takes double damage (crit effect).

    Superiority Die & Maneuvers for Martial Classes#

    Superiority Die Overview#

    • All martial classes (Fighter, Barbarian, Ranger, Paladin, etc.) gain 1 Superiority Die per short/long rest.
    • Superiority Dice are used to fuel maneuvers, which allow martial characters to perform special combat techniques.
    • Battle Masters gain extra Superiority Dice and more maneuvers.

    How Maneuvers Work#

    • At level 3, martial classes pick 2 maneuvers from the list.
    • At levels 5, 7, 9, 11, etc., they unlock new maneuvers.
    • Superiority Dice recover on a short/long rest.
    • Battle Masters get extra Superiority Dice and can use more maneuvers per rest.
